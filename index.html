Services

FSC Ltd leads sustainable agriculture in Rwanda through expertise in agroforestry, waste management, biogas generation, and community-driven solutions, promoting environmental stewardship and resilience in rural economies.

Our Team

Our team at FSC Ltd comprises dedicated professionals advancing sustainable agroforestry and livestock farming, specializing in climate-smart agriculture and innovative solutions like biogas and organic manure production, ensuring top-quality environmental practices for a resilient ecosystem.

Testimonials

Discover what our clients have to say about FSC Ltd, a leader in sustainable agroforestry and forestry. Our mission aims to enhance productivity through eco-friendly practices, promoting crop and livestock value chains while combating food shortages.

FSC Ltd pioneers sustainable agriculture in Rwanda with over 15 years of expertise, focusing on agroforestry, waste management, biogas, and community-driven solutions.
